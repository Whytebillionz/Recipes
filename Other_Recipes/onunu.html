Onunu Recipe

Discription

Onunu is a tasty mouthwatering delicacy from Rivers State in the south-south part of Nigeria. It is the traditional meal of the Ijaw, Kalabari, Degema and Opobo people of Rivers State and Awara/ASA in Imo state.

Onunu is mashed yam and plantain mixed with palm oil and some salt. it is prepared as a smooth mash (not heavy or glutinous dough like pounded yam). the major type of plantain uesd is over-riped plantain.
